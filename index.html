About Us

Building Tomorrow's Engineers

A centre of excellence with world-class facilities and industry-focused curriculum

Narayana Engineering College is an autonomous institution affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Anantapur (JNTUA) and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. Established in 2001, the college has consistently maintained high academic standards and an outstanding placement record.

The campus is equipped with well-furnished laboratories, digital classrooms, a vast library with 50,000+ volumes, high-speed Wi-Fi, and dedicated research centres. The college fosters innovation, technical skills, and holistic development.

  • NAAC Accredited institution with autonomous status
  • Industry-partnered curriculum with live project exposure
  • MoUs with leading IT companies for internships & placements
  • Active student clubs: coding, robotics, entrepreneurship, arts
  • Centrally air-conditioned smart classrooms with digital boards
  • Hostel facilities for boys & girls with 24×7 security

Attendance Policy — NEC Gudur

Minimum Required: 75%

Students must maintain at least 75% attendance in each subject to be eligible for semester examinations (as per JNTUA regulations).

Condonation: 65%–74%

Students with 65–74% attendance may apply for condonation at the department level. Medical certificates & valid reasons required.

Detained: Below 65%

Students below 65% attendance will be detained and cannot appear for semester examinations in that subject.

Attendance Recording

Attendance is recorded every period by faculty. Students can view real-time attendance on the NEC ERP portal and the JNTUA Attendance Portal.
