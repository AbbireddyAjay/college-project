About Us

Building Tomorrow's Engineers

A centre of excellence with world-class facilities and industry-focused curriculum

Narayana Engineering College is an autonomous institution affiliated to JNTUA and approved by AICTE, New Delhi. Established in 2001, the college has consistently maintained high academic standards and an outstanding placement record.

The campus is equipped with well-furnished laboratories, digital classrooms, a vast library with 50,000+ volumes, high-speed Wi-Fi, and dedicated research centres fostering innovation and holistic development.

  • NAAC Accredited institution with autonomous status
  • Industry-partnered curriculum with live project exposure
  • MoUs with leading IT companies for internships & placements
  • Active student clubs: coding, robotics, entrepreneurship
  • Smart classrooms with digital boards throughout campus
  • Hostel facilities for boys & girls with 24×7 security

✅ Attendance Portals

Batch-wise attendance portal links — grouped by admission year

✅ Minimum Required: 75%

Students must maintain at least 75% attendance per subject to be eligible for semester exams — per JNTUA regulations.

⚠️ Condonation: 65% – 74%

Students in this range may apply for condonation at the department. Medical certificates and valid reasons required.

🚫 Detained: Below 65%

Students below 65% are detained and cannot appear for end-semester exams in that subject.
